# Privacy Policy for DevTools

**Last Updated: January 21, 2026**

## Overview

DevTools ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices for the DevTools Chrome extension.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

The Extension operates entirely offline and locally within your browser. All data processing happens on your device.

## Permissions Usage

The Extension requests the following permissions:

### storage
- **Purpose**: Save user preferences (theme selection and language preference)
- **Data Stored**: Only theme choice (dark/light) and language choice (English/Chinese)
- **Location**: Stored locally in Chrome's local storage on your device
- **Sharing**: Never shared or transmitted anywhere

### clipboardWrite
- **Purpose**: Enable one-click copy functionality for tool outputs
- **Usage**: Allows copying formatted results to your clipboard
- **Data Access**: Only writes data you explicitly choose to copy

### clipboardRead
- **Purpose**: Enable paste buttons for quick input
- **Usage**: Allows reading clipboard content when you click paste buttons
- **Data Access**: Only reads when you explicitly click a paste button

## What We Don't Do

- ❌ We do not collect personal information
- ❌ We do not track your usage or behavior
- ❌ We do not use analytics or telemetry
- ❌ We do not share data with third parties
- ❌ We do not display advertisements
- ❌ We do not require account creation or login
- ❌ We do not transmit any data over the network

## Data Processing

All data you input into the Extension's tools (JSON, Base64 strings, passwords, tokens, etc.) is:
- Processed entirely locally in your browser
- Never sent to any external server
- Never stored permanently (except user preferences as noted above)
- Cleared when you close the Extension or refresh the page

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The Extension is designed for developers and does not target children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
